![Request distribution map](~/assets/images/workers/workers-metrics-request-distribution-map.png)

The [Workers Metrics dashboard](https://dash.cloudflare.com/?to=/:account/workers/services/view/:worker/production/metrics/) now includes an interactive request distribution map that shows Worker request throughput by location.

The map shows you locations where your Worker ran in response to incoming requests, and can help you understand where user traffic to your Workers application is coming from. The map also illustrates the power of Cloudflare's global network, and the ability for applications built on Workers to efficiently serve users anywhere in the world.
